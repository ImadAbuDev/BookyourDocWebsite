Support and Documentation of the Website

Welcome to our website!



Here you can quickly and easily book your next doctor appointment.

Registration and usage for doctors:

If you do not yet have an account, you must first click on the "Register" button. A page will then open where you can enter your personal data. Make sure you select doctor for account type. After you have entered all the data, confirm the whole thing with the "Register" button. Now you are registered and should be forwarded directly to the dashboard page (further steps see booking).

Login if you already have an account:

If you already have an account on our site, all you have to do is enter your registration data and click on the "Login" button. Now you are registered and will be forwarded directly to the booking page (for further steps see booking).

Booking and managing appointments with your patients:

You are now on the dashboard side for the doctors. By clicking on management for doc's you can open a selection menu with two buttons. You can select all appointments or only one appointment from the doctor. Then you see all appointments in the table below.
